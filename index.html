THE NOUVEAU STORY

We are a sustainable and carbon neutral denim brand that incorporates up-cycling in our practices. With the option of bringing your own denim, our customers know exactly where their tote bag is from and can confidently wear it, knowing it had no negative impact on the environment.

When you order your totebag, you get to choose between three different options. You can either order a tote made form materials we have in storage, a custom tote from denim materials you provide, or a tote made from a mix of your own denim and our materials.

All of our totebags are made locally and by hand, ensuring safe, sustainable and ethical practices.

The fashion industry today is responsible for 10% of carbon emissions which is more than aviation and shipping industries combined. Nouveau wants to be a part of an important change from fast fashion to conscious fashion. We want to make people excited about recycling their clothing. Because each totebag is made from recycled material, every single one is "one-of-a-kind". We source our materials from thrift stores and recycling donations.
